Vanguard‑01 • Grounded Military Fiction

The 48‑Hour Inspection

What begins as a routine training cycle collapses into a contained warzone. When command integrity fails, a specialized maintenance flight is forced to pivot from wrench‑turners to frontline defenders, leveraging procedure, checklist discipline, and raw improvisation just to see sunrise.

The story is engineered as a tense, single‑location thriller: one base, one night, eighteen maintainers, and a professional adversary who underestimates the lethality of "support" troops who know every inch of the infrastructure.

Mechanical Ingenuity Under Fire

From resurrecting failing water units to sustain the squad, to weaponizing high‑pressure fuel systems and repurposing diagnostic gear as early‑warning devices, every set‑piece leans on plausible technical problem‑solving.

Operational Data

Tactical breakdown of the Flight 221 survival incident — structured so scenes can translate into level design, training sims, or scenario‑driven interactive fiction.

Personnel: The 18 Maintainers

Cross‑functional maintainers with just enough weapons training to be dangerous when cornered — avionics, propulsion, water & fuels, power production, and a crew chief who refuses to surrender the flightline.

Objective: The Water Tower

Control of a fortified vantage point overlooking the dormitories. The tower becomes the pivot: either a killbox controlled by the attackers or a makeshift stronghold controlled by the maintainers.
