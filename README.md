## 📖 Our Methodology: Transmedia Architecture
Historically, the entertainment and technology sectors have been siloed. Teq Vault shatters these boundaries through a systematic pipeline we call **Transmedia Architecture**. 

We do not graft stories onto pre-existing game prototypes. Instead, we architect transmedia universes through full-length manuscripts and story bibles first. Once the lore, physics, and character motivations are mathematically and narratively sound, we hand these immutable blueprints over to our Interactive Entertainment division.

## 🗺️ Institutional Roadmap
Teq Vault operates on a systematic approach to multi-disciplinary market leadership and vertical integration:
* **Phase I (Foundation):** Mobile Games and Distribution. Establishing a direct-to-consumer pipeline and initial fanbase via accessible interactive entertainment.
* **Phase II (Synergy):** Transmedia IP Development. Constructing a deep intellectual property library to create a "Transmedia Flywheel" that lowers user acquisition costs.
* **Phase III (Scaling):** High-Fidelity Porting. Transitioning proven mobile IPs to the enthusiast PC and console markets.
* **Phase IV (Integration):** Electronics Manufacturing. Achieving full vertical integration through proprietary physical technology.
